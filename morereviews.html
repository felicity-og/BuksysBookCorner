Part of Your World by Abby Jimenez

Usually it takes some time for me to fully fall in love with a book boyfriend but with Daniel Grant, it came so easily. From the first few chapters, you get an idea of the kind of human he is, kind, humble and someone that reignites your faith in mankind ( I might be exaggerating but if you've read it, tell me you understand!!).
